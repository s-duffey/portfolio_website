How It Works

Get a Quote for Your Project

Provide your details online using the client information form. I will send you an email with a quote for your project within 48 hours.

Book Your Discovery Call

Pick a time to chat. During this call we will discuss your style, your business and sign a contact for the project.

Design Your Custom Package

Based on the number of products and where you will be using your images, we will create a custom package for your project. This includes a draft shot list.

Send Your Products

Ship your products to my studio. If you are in Ottawa, we can also arrange a delivery or pickup or shoot at your location.

Choose Your Images for Editing

Once the images are ready, I will send you an online proof gallery. Using the gallery, you can choose your favourites to use as your final images. At this point, it's ok to choose more than we originally planned for. You will be sent an invoice once you choose your final images.

Recieve Your Final Images

I will edit your images and send the high-resolution files to you through your gallery. Depending on the purpose of your images, I can also send custom sizes (e.g. for web). Your images are now ready to use!
