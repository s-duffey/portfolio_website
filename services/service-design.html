Content Design

Content design is the deliberate and expert planning and creation of content (writing, video, audio etc.). When content is properly designed it becomes much more efficient, scalable and high quality. Content is king, so content design is important to use your content to its full potential, re-use it in new ways, and save tremendous amounts of time and energy.

Inventories and audits

Before you can make your content work harder, it’s important to break down each piece and evaluate its quality and connections. Content audits take stock of what you have before redesigning it to work better.

Final result: Content inventory

Content management and re-use

Next we’ll look at managing your content more efficiently, making sure you have access to all your individual content pieces anywhere, anytime inspiration strikes. This includes tagging and categorizing so you can easily find what you need.

Final result: new folder structure, content plan, metadata cheatsheet

Editing and reviews

Content designers can figure out which pieces need some editing and testing. Reviews can focus on plain language, accessibility, website usability and web navigation. Different testing techniques can help you understand which style and format works best for your audience.

Final result: website review, edited content

Content creation and guidance

This phase involves creating new content to fill in the gaps or create something entirely new. Creating a set of guidelines or style standards will help with content creation in the future, by keeping content consistent with your brand and making each piece re-usable for different formats.

Final result: Content style guide, new content
