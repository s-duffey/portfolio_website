Portrait Photography

Personal portraits for branding, couples, candids, passion projects and more. Let's capture your story through the lens. Sessions can be done in-studio or on-location. Get in touch so we can chat about your style!

Portrait sessions include:

  • 30 minute phone call or prep session
  • Minimum 1 hour of shooting in-studio or at a location of your choice
  • 15+ edited, high-resolution images
  • Shareable online gallery
  • Viewing session
  • $50 credit for print products of your choice
